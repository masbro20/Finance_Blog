How can a person be truly happy?

  1. Take care of physical health
    • eat healthy
    • exercise regularly
    • get enough sleep
  2. Maintain healthy relationships
    • Love is spelled TIME
    • Enjoy each moment (you never know how many you have left with them)
  3. Spiritual Resilience
    • Scipture study with real intent to listen to the Spirit during study
    • Prayer with sincere intent to act on impressions and listen frequently for directions
    • Consistently keeping covenants and strong bond with Heavenly Father

    4. If anything is getting in the way of the above, STOP IT.
